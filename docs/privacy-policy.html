Overview

FiberPhoto ("the App") is a field documentation tool for recording and photographing fiber optic infrastructure. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Short version: FiberPhoto stores all data locally on your device. No personal information is transmitted to external servers.

Information We Collect Local Only

The App collects and stores the following information on your device only:

  • Technician name – entered by the user to identify who recorded each structure. Stored in the on-device SQLite database.
  • Photos – photographs taken through the App are saved to your device's local file storage.
  • Structure records – metadata associated with fiber structures (type, sequence number, location designations, notes). Stored in a local SQLite database.
  • App settings – preferences such as starting sequence numbers. Stored locally.

None of this data leaves your device in the current version of the App.

Device Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera – required to photograph fiber structures. Photos are saved locally.
  • Storage / File System – required to save and read photos and database files on your device.

Permissions are only used for the core functionality of the App and are never used to collect personal data.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your data to third parties.
  • Share your data with advertisers.
  • Send any App data to external servers or cloud services.
  • Use any analytics SDKs or tracking libraries.

The only time data leaves your device is when you choose to export a ZIP archive using the built-in export feature and share it manually via your device's share dialog.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and your device's standard file system. Security of locally stored data is subject to your device's own security model (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend using a screen lock on your device.

Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained on your device until you delete it within the App or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App will remove the database. Photos saved to device storage may remain until deleted manually.

Children's Privacy

FiberPhoto is intended for use by adults in professional field environments. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Future Changes

Future versions of the App may introduce optional cloud synchronisation features (e.g., to sync records across devices). If and when such features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe what data is transmitted, how it is stored, and how it can be deleted. Users will be notified of material changes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
